Appearing on the Sidehustle Success podcast

I recently had the opportunity to sit down with Steve Haunts for the Side Hustle Success podcast and talk about quitting my job to go freelance full-time. Amongst other things we talked about freelancing as lifestyle, running a 'company of one', and taking care of your body and mind when you're on your own out in the wilds!

It was a pleasure to take an hour out and chat with Steve - he's a great host - and I hope that the conversation we had helps shed some light on the freelance life, answering some questions (or asking even more!) for anyone who is considering making the switch.
