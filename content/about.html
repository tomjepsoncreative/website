Hi, I'm Tom.

I'm a Nottingham-based freelance designer specialising in user experience, web, and app design. I love my work, meeting new people, and discovering new ways to achieve great results. There are no 'hard and fast' rules to getting the job done; I aim for happy clients and flexible working over hours spent behind a screen.

When I work with clients, I focus on strategies to expose the 'why' - user needs and pain points, business goals and expectations, improvable metrics - that leads the 'what' and the 'how' of a solution. My goal is to create great products with real people at their heart.

An advocate for remote work, I've been a designer in various capacities for the last decade and am now working as a freelancer full-time; my last role was Head of Design for a digital health company. I've worked in various industries including telecoms and marketing as an in-house designer, and have worked under my own name since 2011.
