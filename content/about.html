Hi, I'm Tom.

I'm a Nottingham-based freelance designer specialising in user experience, web, and app design; my focus is creating great products and solutions with real people at their heart. When working with clients, I am strategically-focussed and look to expose the ‘why’ - user needs and pain points, business goals and expectations, improvable metrics - that leads the ‘what’ and the ‘how’.

An advocate for remote work, I’ve been a designer in various capacities for the last decade and am now working as a freelancer full-time; my last role was Head of Design for a digital health company. I’ve worked in various industries including telecoms and marketing. I have worked with the following business as an in-house designer and have worked under my own name since 2011.

  • History
  • 2018 - 2019: Biotaware, Nottingham, UK (Head of Design)
  • 2016 - 2018: Buying Butler, Nottingham, UK (Lead Designer)
  • 2013 - 2016: Esendex, Nottingham, UK (Designer)
  • 2008 - 2013: Vision Express, Nottingham, UK (Communications Manager, Designer)

In my leadership roles, I’ve taken great pride in mentoring and guiding less experienced team members, providing contextually-appropriate processes and methods to help educate teams to achieve their best work. My personal bar is pretty damn high, too, so I hold only the highest standards for the experiences that are delivered to users of the products and solutions I create.
